Post Tags

Mathematics

The maths tag encapsulates pages/posts on general maths topics, including real/complex analysis, Lie group/algebra, and manifolds.

Statistics

The statistics tag provides a full list of pages/posts ranging from fundamental probabilistic theory, stochastic processes to sophisticated bayesian inference.

Data Structures

The data structures tag retrieves all pages/posts on data structures required by classical and advanced algorithms.

Software Packages

The software packages tag contains pages/posts on not-so-popular software packages that may assist in scientific research.

Algorithms

The algorithms tag maintains an assorted pages/posts list including sequential, parallel, and distributed.

Hacker's Delight

The hacker's delight tag gets its name from an excellent book. It collects pages/posts that demonstrate attacks on tricky coding puzzles.

Machine Learning

The machine learning tag encapsulates pages/posts on general machine learning methods, such as decision trees, kernel methods, and clusters.

Book Reviews

The book reviews tag collects pages/posts on book reviews to a much broader extent than academia.

Miscellaneous

The miscellaneous tag keeps a list of pages/posts that do not fall under categories of other tags.

Deep Learning

The deep learning tag gathers pages/posts that are primarily concerned with techniques of the epoch-making resurgence of connectionism since 2012.

Foreign Languages

The foreign languages tag keeps track of pages/posts that document seminal (yet unprofessional) views in the fond acquisition of foreign languages.

Welcome

The welcome tag maintains an infrequently updated list of pages/posts that are intended for new readers to this site.

Personal Life

The personal life tag keeps track of pages/posts on the author's life experiences other than academia.
